Preserving Our Parks

How You Can Help Preserve Our National Parks

Preserving our national parks is a collective effort. Here are a few ways you can contribute:

  • Practice "Leave No Trace" principles.
  • Participate in volunteer programs.
  • Support park conservation initiatives.
  • Stay on designated trails to protect wildlife and vegetation.
  • Report any environmental concerns or illegal activities.
